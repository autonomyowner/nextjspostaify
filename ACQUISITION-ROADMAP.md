### WEEK 1: Foundation & First Users

#### Day 1: Research & Setup
**Time: 5 hours**

- [ ] **Find 20 Facebook Groups** (2 hours)
  - Search terms: "life coach community", "business coaches", "online coaches", "fitness coaches business", "coach entrepreneurs"
  - Target: 5,000-50,000 members (active but manageable)
  - Request to join ALL 20

- [ ] **Optimize your personal Facebook profile** (30 min)
  - Professional photo
  - Cover photo mentioning POSTAIFY or content creation
  - Bio: "Helping coaches save 8+ hours/week on content creation"
  - Add POSTAIFY waitlist link

- [ ] **Optimize LinkedIn profile** (30 min)
  - Headline: "Founder @ POSTAIFY | AI Content Tool for Coaches"
  - About section: Your story + the problem you solve
  - Featured: Link to POSTAIFY

- [ ] **Create a simple Loom video** (1 hour)
  - 2-3 minute demo of POSTAIFY
  - Show: Creating content in under 5 minutes
  - No fancy editing needed, authentic > polished

- [ ] **Prepare swipe file** (1 hour)
  - Save the DM scripts from this doc
  - Save the post templates
  - Bookmark competitor complaints/reviews for talking points

#### Day 2: Enter the Groups
**Time: 5 hours**

- [ ] **Introduce yourself in accepted groups** (1 hour)
  - Don't pitch! Just say hi
  - Example: "Hey everyone! [Name] here, just joined. Love this community. I help coaches with content creation. Excited to learn from you all!"

- [ ] **Study the groups** (2 hours)
  - What questions get asked most?
  - Who are the active members?
  - What are the group rules?
  - What posts get the most engagement?

- [ ] **Find 20 "pain point" posts** (1 hour)
  - Search in groups: "content", "posting", "social media", "overwhelmed", "ideas"
  - Screenshot and save posts where coaches complain about content creation
  - Note down the usernames

- [ ] **Comment on 15 posts with value** (1 hour)
  - No pitching, just help
  - Share tips, encouragement, your experience
  - Goal: Be seen as helpful community member

#### Day 3: Start Outreach
**Time: 5 hours**

- [ ] **Send first 10 DMs** (2 hours)
  - Use the DM Script #1 (see Scripts section)
  - Target people who posted about content struggles
  - Personalize each message (mention their specific post)

- [ ] **Comment on 20 posts** (1 hour)
  - Continue adding value
  - Reply to comments on your previous comments
  - Build relationships

- [ ] **Create first value post** (1 hour)
  - Use Post Template #1 (see Scripts section)
  - Post in 2-3 groups
  - Topic: "5 content ideas for coaches this week"

- [ ] **LinkedIn: Connect with 20 coaches** (1 hour)
  - Search: "life coach", "business coach", "mindset coach"
  - Send personalized connection requests
  - Don't pitch in the connection request!

#### Day 4: Follow Up & Scale
**Time: 5 hours**

- [ ] **Follow up on Day 3 DMs** (1 hour)
  - If no reply after 24h, send follow-up
  - Use Follow-up Script (see Scripts section)

- [ ] **Send 15 new DMs** (2 hours)
  - Mix of Facebook and LinkedIn
  - Target new pain-point posters

- [ ] **Comment on 25 posts** (1 hour)
  - You should be getting recognized now
  - Reply to people who replied to you

- [ ] **Post in 3 new groups** (1 hour)
  - Different value post than Day 3
  - Or same post if it performed well

#### Day 5: Get First Free Users
**Time: 5 hours**

- [ ] **Review all conversations** (1 hour)

## Emergency Pivots

### If DMs aren't working (< 20% reply rate):
1. Test different opening lines
2. Make messages shorter
3. Add more personalization
4. Try different times of day
5. Focus on LinkedIn instead

### If Facebook Ads aren't working (CPA > $50):
1. Kill underperforming ads after 3 days
2. Test completely new creative (video vs image)
3. Narrow audience (coaches only, not general)
4. Try Instagram-only placement
5. Shift budget to influencers

### If free users aren't converting (< 5%):
1. Check if they're actually using the product
2. Add more personal touchpoints
3. Offer extended trial
4. Add better onboarding
5. Ask why (feedback call)

### If stuck at 50 customers:
1. Launch referral program with better incentive
2. Test new niche (fitness coaches, real estate)
3. Create free lead magnet for top of funnel
4. Guest on podcasts
5. Partner with coach communities
